Future Events

Rehmat Foundation in collaboration with PIMS in Islamabad and Nishtar Medical University in Multan will hold medical conferences in Pakistan in 2024. The conferences will focus on mental health, substance abuse, allergy/ Immunology, and Stem cells. 1st conference will take place in Islamabad on February 12-13 and 2nd conference will be in Multan on February 19-20.

Free Medical Camp

Free medical camp in collaboration with Nishtar Medical University will be held at THQ Hospital Jahania on February 15, 2024. Various medical specialists from Nishtar and the USA will provide services.

The Board of Rehmat Foundation will Tour the schools in Jahania on February 16, 2024
Rehmat Foundation will have a meeting with educators, parents, students, and community leaders in Jahania on February 17, 2024

Hands on work shop in cardiology at PIMS Islamabad by Dr Sohail Hassan of Wayne State University, School of Medicine, arranged by Rehmat Foundation on January 18, 2024.
