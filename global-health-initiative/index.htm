Global Health Initiative

Global Health Initiative

Rehmat Foundation Global Medicine Initiative strives to facilitate medical education, research, student and scholar exchange between institutions in Pakistan and individuals/institutions in USA to improve healthcare in Pakistan.
We have about 90 medical experts in various specialties who deliver lectures at several medical institutions in Pakistan via Zoom.

Rehmat Foundation facilitates biweekly grand rounds in pediatrics at Wayne State University, School of Medicine, and our partners in pediatrics in Pakistan.

Rehmat Foundation funded a training and research program “Building Baby Brains” for 55 lady health workers in rural Multan through Supporting Child Givers Organization in Massachusetts. Rehmat Foundation Facilitated a training Program in Dermatology for dermatologists from Pakistan with Hamzvi Dermatology Group in the state of Michigan.

 

Our Speaker Series

This program focuses on sharing/exchange of the latest information and collaborative research in the field of Medicine between individuals/institutions in the USA and Pakistan to improve healthcare.
