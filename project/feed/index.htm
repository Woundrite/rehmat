]]> Infectious diseases are preventable and treatable . Unfortunately in undeveloped countries an estimated 5 million children under the age of 5 died in 2020 according to WHO . Pakistan is one of the countries with highest child mortality rate .According to UNICEF 65.2 out of 1000 live births die before age 5 in Pakistan.
To tackle this issue, Rehmat Foundation has partnered with ChildLife Foundation to develop a program at Tehsil Headquarters Hospital in Jahania where seriously ill children are being treated daily. It has also funded 5 ER beds at the Children Hospital, Multan.

]]> Rehmat Foundation shares the views of Nelson Mandela who said “Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world”. Poverty has a big impact on child education, mental stability, and future economic independence.

In Pakistan, school children who live in poverty face many obstacles, one of them is the inability to buy school uniforms and supplies. Therefore Rehmat Foundation in collaboration with Light4Life provided 550 students with uniforms and school supplies to prevent dropout in Maitla Chowk area, Pakistan. In addition, Rehmat Foundation has provided school uniforms to another 231 students for year 2023.
